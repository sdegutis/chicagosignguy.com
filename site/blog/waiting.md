I drew this girl in my sketchbook this morning.
I've tried to reproduce her on an index card several times, but I can't.

I am not quite sure what inspired it.
I think it must be the Blessed Virgin Mary, the day after Jesus died.
She spent all Saturday longing for Easter Sunday, sad and waiting.

I think it's also a portrait of my soul.
In Catholic theology, each person's soul is the same gender as their body.
But in another sense---in relation to Jesus---all souls are female.

I pray every day. But God has not yet answered any of my prayers.
I hope he will, one day. But for now, I am waiting.

I named this drawing *waiting*.
I drew it while watching this [recent Jacob Collier video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=BFul90BFjGc). Life is so beautiful.

I will be in Chicago on Easter, and then gone for a while.
I trust none of you will miss me too much while waiting.
