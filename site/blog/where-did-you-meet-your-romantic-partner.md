School won!

## Takwaways

You can meet a romantic partner anywhere.

School is the most common place to find one,
probably because young people are eager to find love.

Work is last place,
I'm guessing because
dating in the workplace is just plain messy,
and often forbidden.

Random places in public
are not out of the question!
If you see someone you think you might like,
say hi to them!

Mutuals are a great way to meet someone.
Try hanging out with tangent friend groups.

Dating apps seem to have a stigma,
but should they?
