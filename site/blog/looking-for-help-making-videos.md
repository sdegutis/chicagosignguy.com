But I think that ship has sailed.

I think a video format works *a lot* better for this kind of thing:

* It's easier to see and hear people's answers than me remembering them.
* Everyone's already on social media.
* Nobody reads blogs.
* It's already a lot of work to maintain a blog website.
* I'm getting so burnt out running this website.
* I still have writers block, so I need an alternate way to make money.

So, I'm looking for help making videos.

What I need specifically:

* Someone to manage the Instagram, TikTok, and YouTube pages.
* Someone to record videos.

I'm still on the fence as to whether this is ethical.
I still don't like social media and don't want to feed people's addictions.

So I'll just leave it in God's hands.
If it works, and I find someone to do this with, then fine.
If not, also fine.
