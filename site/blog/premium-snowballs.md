I made the highest quality snowballs,
out of premium snow taken by the garden near the Chicago Theater,
and sold them for $1 each.

These were not your grandpa's snowballs,
these babies do not crumble when you hold them or throw them.
Worth every penny, in fact they're normally $1,000 each,
but it was a holiday sale.
