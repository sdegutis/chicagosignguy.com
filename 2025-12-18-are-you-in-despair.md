---
title: Are you in despair?
---

This is an earlier sign I did, about mid November, which is why it's such thin marker and hard to read. Because of that, I don't remember many of the specific answers.

I intentionally left the question vague and put only four vague answers. I'm surprised how many people put Kinda, it's pretty much tied with None.

For most of the people who put 100%, I would say something along the lines of, "really? are you being honest?" One of them was a young woman with her friend, and as she walked away fairly cheerfully, she said "yes, last week I tried to kill myself!" And she practically skipped away.

I think a few of those who said they were at 100% had heart aches from heart breaks, and admitted that they knew they were going to get better.
